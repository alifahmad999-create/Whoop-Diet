How the Calorie Bank works
Every day you complete adjusts the balance by target − eaten. Finish under target and the leftover is added; finish over target and the difference is subtracted.
  • It's a real running net, not a one-way total — over-target days pull the balance back down, they don't just get ignored
  • The balance can go negative. That's not a lockout, it just means you've eaten more than your targets lately — a day or two under target brings it back toward zero
  • Today's numbers don't count yet — a day only settles once it rolls over at midnight
  • 7,700 kcal ≈ 1 kg of fat-loss equivalent — your balance is measured against that to reach each milestone
  • Once you've banked a healthy cushion, this card will suggest a treat-sized amount you can spend without undoing your progress
A cumulative estimate of fat-loss progress — not guaranteed weight loss. The balance can run negative on over-target days; banked flexibility is yours to spend on a treat or spread over a few days.
